LES also offers for sale a two-color, laser-interferometer diagnostic available in two configurations - as a 1-dimensional, line-of-sight instrument and a 2-dimensional, transverse-imaging instrument. This interferometer design is robustly stable with a broad range in sensitivity and can be used to quantitatively measure density as a dispersive phase shift in plasma, gas, and liquids. Dispersion interferometry is a tremendous simplification over conventional methods in terms of its simplicity, performance, and cost.
