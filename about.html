About:

    L-Egant Solutions (LES) provides cost-effective, high-voltage systems, components, and diagnostics for applications in pulsed-power and plasma-physics research, typically for for switching, triggering, charging, controlling, high voltage pulses, etc. Most of these products are standard designs, although custom designs are also available, so don't hesitate to ask if you don't see what you need.

    The Second-Harmonic Dispersion Interferometer (SHDI) is our latest offering. The SHDI is typically used to measure the density in plasma, gas, and liquids. The instrument is easy to use and robustly stable and comes in two configurations: one used to measure the density along a 1-dimensional line-of-sight path, and another used to measure a 2-dimensional, transverse-density profiles.

    The quality of these products is backed by years of experience and iterative development. Over the past decades my research has been in physics studying the injection of intense-ion beams into a fusion plasma, ion-space propulsion, fusion in a Dense Z-pinch and in a Magnetic, Field-Reversed Configuration. Along the way I co-founded several start-ups, including: Tri Alpha Energy, Inc. and Magneto-Inertial Fusion Technologies, Inc., and (most recently) SAFEnergy, Inc.

    It will be my pleasure to provide you with the highest level of support that can facilitate your research, efficiently and at low cost.
